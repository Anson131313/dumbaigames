Our Story

More Than Just a Haircut

Founded in 2015, Elite Cuts was built on a simple idea: every man deserves to look and feel his best. What started as a single chair in East Nashville has grown into one of the city's most respected grooming destinations.

Our barbers bring over 30 years of combined experience, blending time-honored techniques with contemporary style. We believe a great cut starts with listening — understanding your lifestyle, your preferences, and your vision.

Step in, grab a cold drink, and let us take care of the rest.
