Born in Nashville,
Raised on Smoke

Smoky Roots BBQ started with a simple belief: great barbecue doesn't need shortcuts. In 2014, pitmaster Marcus "Big M" Calloway fired up his first offset smoker in a converted garage on Dickerson Pike with nothing but family recipes and a whole lot of patience.

Every brisket is smoked for 14 hours over Tennessee hickory. Every sauce is made from scratch — our signature Smoky Roots Red blends brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, and just enough cayenne to remind you where you are. We cure our own bacon, pickle our own okra, and bake our cornbread in cast iron.

What started as weekend pop-ups at the Nashville Farmers' Market grew into the brick-and-mortar you know today — a place where families gather, live blues fills the room, and every plate tells a story.

— Marcus & Diane Calloway, Founders
