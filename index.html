What even is this? 🤔

This is DumbAIGames.com — a collection of browser games built by a 12-year-old from Nashville and his AI assistant.

Every game on this site was coded from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No game engines. No templates. Just vibes and late nights.

Are they perfect? No. Are they dumb? A little. Are they fun? That's for you to decide. 🎮
